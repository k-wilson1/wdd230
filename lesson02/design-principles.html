White Space and Clean Design

Nike.com

Nike.com uses white space and a clean design to attract customers to their products.

Contrast

FlyNashville.com

At Flynashville.com the use of contrasting colors is used to attract customers and highlight their logo. It uses bright yellow and dark blue.

Rule of thirds

DisneyPlus.com

Disneyplus has a very busy page but uses the Rules of thirds with each section highlighting a different area of interest.
