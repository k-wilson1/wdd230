Richfield Chamber of Commerce

Color Scheme

Our color scheme will be a combination of the following colors:

  • Red
  • White
  • Black
  • Yellow

Typography

Our site will use the following fonts:

  • Accent- Alfa Slab One
  • Primary- Smooth Sans
  • Secondary- Nunito

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide the public with a place to go for updated community information. It is also a place to find out about local events and support local businesses. We will also include a community member highlight to express outstanding citizens.

Scenarios

Our primary audience is the local community. They will be looking for information on local events, businesses, and community members. Our secondary audience is tourists. They will be looking for information on local events and businesses.
